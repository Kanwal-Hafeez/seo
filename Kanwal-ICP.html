Reading between the lines

What This Means for Outreach

Revenue-rich, margin-poor. The top line looks fine. What keeps him up is what's left after rent, equipment, and payroll clear.

Time-poor, not idea-poor. He's in the chair treating patients all day, not behind a desk running campaigns.

Once bitten. His own words draw a line between "dentist" and "marketing expert," the language of someone overpromised to before.

Buys outcomes, not services. He doesn't want a plan on a slide. He wants fewer empty chairs next month.
